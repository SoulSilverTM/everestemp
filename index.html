Frequently Asked Questions

We answer your main questions before starting your project.

Do you work nationwide or just regionally?

We have the logistics and infrastructure to develop and install projects nationwide. Please contact us to review viability at your specific location.

Do you offer warranties on roofing projects?

Yes, we guarantee our work. The warranty period varies depending on the materials and scope of the roofing system (usually between 10 and 20 years for full waterproofing).

Do your welders have certifications?

Absolutely. Our entire team holds international certifications (like AWS) and our company operates under ISO 9001 quality and safety management standards.
